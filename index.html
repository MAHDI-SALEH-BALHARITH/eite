About Us

Energy Innovation Trading Est.Born of a Saudi Arabian heritage that spans 3+ years, EITE contracting was established in the Saudi Arabia in 2016 as a base of operations to launch into the growing Saudi Arabian markets with our specialist Trading and construction techniques and services. EITE’s foundation is based on industry leading and international construction techniques and processes. EITE contracting today operates using the same cutting edge construction techniques and management standards which continue to deliver success for all our clients. With growing necessity to deliver high quality yet cost effective construction solutions to the market EITE Trading and contracting is well positioned to offer a range of value engineering and construction services to all our clients combined with the talented management team that ensure successful delivery of all our projects.
